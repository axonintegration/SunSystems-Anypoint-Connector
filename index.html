SunSystems SSC Anypoint™ Connector

Connects to Infor SunSystems through the integration layer provided called SunSystems Connect (SSC).

Welcome to the SunSystems SSC Anypoint™ Connector Page.

Infor SunSystems is a financial management, purchasing, inventory and sales management solution. The SunSystems SSC Anypoint Connector connectors to Infor SunSystems through the integration layer provided called SunSystems Connect (SSC). This connector provides a wrapper around the SSC functionality to enable the utilization of SSC within the Mule environment.

The SunSystems SSC Anypoint™ Connector is developed to be used within the Anypoint™ platform provided by MuleSoft and is compatible with Mule Runtime 3.6.x
